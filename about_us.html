About Us

At Icon Fresco, we are dedicated to transforming spaces into ambience-enriched environments that captivate and inspire. Based in Hyderabad, we specialize in bringing the finest decorative solutions to life, ensuring each project exudes luxury, comfort, and sophistication. Our approach is centered on customization, creativity, and expert craftsmanship, allowing us to design unique finishes that reflect your personal style and vision.

Every texture and color we offer is carefully chosen to create sensory atmospheres that make a lasting impression. Inspired by classic aesthetics and modern innovation, we aim to elevate interior spaces, crafting experiences that are not only visually stunning but also deeply immersive. At Icon Fresco, we believe in the power of design to enrich lives, and our mission is to redefine interiors with products that blend elegance, functionality, and artistic expression.

Our Vision: Creating Space That Speak

Our finishes are more than just a coat of paint—they are a statement. Each product is designed to enhance the ambience of your space, adding layers of sophistication and comfort. By focusing on both the aesthetic and functional aspects, we ensure that every project delivers a sense of luxury and a feeling of being truly immersed in the moment.

Icon Fresco & Spiver: A Perfect Collaboration

Our collaboration with Spiver, an esteemed Italian leader in the decorative paint industry, brings over 50 years of craftsmanship and innovation to our clients in Hyderabad. This partnership was born out of a shared vision for quality and a passion for creating unique, high-end finishes. By combining Spiver's expertise and rich Italian heritage with our reginal insights and dedication, we are able to offer clients an unparalleled range of decorative solutions that enrich the atmosphere of any space.

Spiver's dedication to excellence and sustainability aligns seamlessly with our values at Icon Fresco. Their renowned products, including the ARTHE line, are celebrated worldwide for their ability to evoke emotion and create lifestyle-defining spaces. Through our partnership, we ensure that the finest elements of Italian elegance are integrated into our designs, bringing a touch of global luxury to Hyderabad.
